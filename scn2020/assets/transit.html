Transit Scooter Hub

These scooter stations enable seamless transfers between scooter, bus, and train. Docks should be placed in close proximity to a transit station. Given the relatively permanent nature of transit infrastructure, it may be best to use permanent docks that draw from electric utilities. Additionally, rebalancing resources should be focused on these stations, as these stations will likely provide a significant portion of ridership for a last-mile scooter system.
