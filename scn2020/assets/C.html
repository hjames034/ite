Design C: Protected Bike Lane with 60'+ Right of way

Design features:

  1. Protected intersections(at intersections with lower traffic volume)
  2. Parking Protected Bike Lane on one side, barrier protected on other side(this can alternate based on side with higher parking demand)
  3. Narrowed Lanes(reduce speed limit to 25)
  4. Bike lane geometry accomadtes intersections with large turning volumes
  5. At bus stops, shared bike/boarding platform
