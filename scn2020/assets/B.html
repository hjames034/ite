Design B: Protected Bike Lane with 75'+ Right of way

Design features:

  1. Protected intersections(at intersections with lower traffic volume)
  2. Parking Protected Bike Lane
  3. Narrowed Lanes(reduce speed limit to 25)
  4. wide sidewalks
  5. Accomadation for higher traffic volumes with rush hour parking/travel flex lane
  6. At bus stops, shared bike/boarding platform
